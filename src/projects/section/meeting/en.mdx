### Preview

- SiDating is a meeting and dating project for students of the University of Seoul, Kyung Hee University, and Hankuk University of Foreign Studies.
- It progressed up to season 6, and I was responsible for server development from season 1 to season 4.

### Tech Stack

- Season 1: Flask, SQLAlchemy
- Season 2: Nest.js
- Season 3: Spring, Kubernetes
- Season 4: Algorithm (Implementation of the Hospital-Resident algorithm)
